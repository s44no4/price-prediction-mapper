Info

Controls

  • Use Current price and Strike price inputs to set the underlying price and the binary strike.
  • Drag the Current and Strike horizontal lines on the Price Distribution chart to update those prices directly.
  • Drag either +1 sigma or -1 sigma point on the Price Distribution chart to change Sigma. Both points control the same sigma value.
  • Use the Sigma slider or number input for exact sigma values from 1 to 1000 bps.
  • Use the T slider below, or drag the T point on the top axis of the Prediction Distribution chart.
  • Use the mouse wheel over the left bps axis or right price axis of the Price Distribution chart to zoom the visible price range.
  • Drag the right price axis on the Price Distribution chart to pan the visible price range up or down.

Formulas and Mapping

  1. Prices are modeled in log-bps space: price = current * exp(bps / 10000).
  2. The plotted remaining volatility is sigma_plot = sigma * sqrt(1 - T).
  3. The Price Distribution chart draws the selected density family over bps using sigma_plot: Gaussian, Student-t, Laplacian, or Logistic. Non-Gaussian families are standardized so sigma remains the bps standard deviation.
  4. The fair binary prediction price is 100 * (1 - F(strike_bps / sigma_plot)), where F is the selected distribution CDF.
  5. The Prediction Distribution uses an analytic change of variables from underlying bps to binary fair price, so it does not depend on the visible chart scale.
  6. The resulting density is evaluated over 0c to 100c and normalized so the total area under the curve is 1.
